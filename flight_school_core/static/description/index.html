Flight School Core

Military Flight School Management System

Core module for managing military flight training following EU JAR-FCL (EASA) regulations.

Key Features

Training Class Types

Define and manage training programs: PPL, CPL, IR, Multi-Engine, and military training courses.

Person Types

Classify people: students, flight instructors, ground instructors, and support staff.

Military Ranks

Configure military rank hierarchy for cadets, officers, NCOs, and civilian contractors.

Role-Based Access

Four access levels: User, Instructor, Manager, and Administrator.

Regulatory Compliance

Designed for compliance with EU JAR-FCL / EASA regulations for pilot training.

  • JAR-FCL license references
  • Flight hour requirements tracking
  • Prerequisite training management
